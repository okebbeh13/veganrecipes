Chickpea Tomato Soup, a delicious and hearty meal!

HOME

This soup is packed with flavor and nutrients, making it a perfect choice for a comforting meal. The combination of chickpeas and tomatoes creates a rich and satisfying dish that is both delicious and nutritious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

Follow these steps to make a savory Chickpea Tomato Soup:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the ground cumin, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using) to the pot. Stir to coat the onions and garlic with the spices.
  3. Add the chickpeas, diced tomatoes (with their juice), and vegetable broth to the pot. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If you prefer a smoother soup, you can use an immersion blender to puree part of the soup directly in the pot, or transfer a portion of it to a blender and blend until smooth before returning it to the pot.
  6. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy!
